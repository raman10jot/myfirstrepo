Origin

Coffee originated in Ethiopia, but is now grown in many countries, including South America, Central America, and Southeast Asia. The earliest evidence of coffee drinking dates back to the 15th century in Yemen.

Production

Coffee is made from the seeds of the coffee plant, which are called coffee beans. The beans are dried and then roasted to develop their flavor and aroma.

Types of coffee

The two most common types of coffee beans are Arabica and Robusta. Arabica beans are more flavorful and aromatic, while Robusta beans are hardier and have twice the caffeine content of Arabica.Caffeine,Nutrients,Word origin

Preparation

Coffee is typically brewed by steeping ground coffee beans in hot water and then filtering out the beans. Coffee can be served in many ways, including espresso, cappuccino, mocha, and latte.

Coffee as a good

Coffee is usually brewed immediately before drinking. In most areas, coffee may be purchased unprocessed, or already roasted, or already roasted and ground. Coffee is often vacuum packed to prevent oxidation and lengthen its shelf life.
